The School

History of BVM

'Challenges are what make life interesting and overcoming them is what makes life meaningful' - Joshua J.

Established in the year 2013, Bharath Vidya Mandir began its journey with 449 students and 35 teachers with classes till Std. 6. Sheer hard work and passion for excellence has allowed the school to flourish and now it is brimming with 750 students studying till class 10 and 51 teachers. The school is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and follows the CCE pattern of education.

With its motto as 'Ignite the potential', the school believes that every child has inherent ability to succeed and that he/she must be carefully oriented towards it. The school ignites the innate potential in the students and allows them to proceed towards excellence. The school understands that the harmonious development of a child is more valuable than pure academic success and therefore undertakes numerous programmes in ensuring that this transcends into reality.
